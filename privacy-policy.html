TransitFX — Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 15, 2026

Data Collection

TransitFX does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data. No analytics, telemetry, or tracking of any kind is used.

Local Storage

The extension stores your preferences (selected effect, theme, speed, site rules) locally using the browser's chrome.storage.local API. This data never leaves your device.

Permissions

TransitFX requests only the storage permission, which is used to persist your settings across sessions.

Third-Party Services

TransitFX does not communicate with any external servers or third-party services.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, the changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
